Hexophen is a hexagonal chess variant played on a 91 hexagonal cells board.

Each player commands 21 pieces

  • 1 king
  • 1 queen
  • 2 rooks
  • 3 bishops
  • 3 knights
  • 11 pawns

Initial setup

There is no castling. When a pawn advances to the ranks behind the oponent's pawns' initial position only on files c-i, as part of that move it is promoted for the player's choice of queen, rook, bishop or knight.
