Jocly Makruk cheat sheet

Pieces

White and black pieces for 2D and 3D versions (from left to right):

  • Pawn (8 per player)
  • Knight (2 per player)
  • Bishop (2 per player)
  • Rook (2 per player)
  • Queen (1 per player)
  • King (1 per player)

As for international chess you have to mate the opponent's king but pieces have different shapes and some have different moves, especially the queen (much weaker) and the bishop.
