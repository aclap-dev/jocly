This game is a variant of Tamerlane II. itself inspired from "Ajâ'ib al-maqdûr fî qissat Timûr" : The wonders of destiny in the story of Tamerlane.

Home page concerning Timurid chess

Choose your variant in the familly

The first step is to choose which variant of the Timurid family you want to play : you can choose

  • Lion, Queen and Snake for the central piece
  • Boats and Griffins for the side rooms

Choosing the griffins will result in a more aggressive and undoubtedly faster game..
