Choose your variant in the familly

The first step is to choose which variant of the Timurid family you want to play : you can choose

  • Lion, Queen and Snake for the central piece
  • Boats and Griffons for the side rooms

Choosing the griffons will result in a more aggressive and undoubtedly faster game. Choosing the ship will result in a more aggressive and undoubtedly faster game. The choice of ship will result in a game that favors the quest for promotion.
