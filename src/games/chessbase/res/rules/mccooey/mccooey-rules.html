Mac Cooey's chess is a hexagonal chess variant played on a 91 hexagonal cells board.

Each player commands 16 pieces

  • 1 king
  • 1 queen
  • 2 rooks
  • 3 bishops
  • 2 knights
  • 7 pawns

Initial setup

There is no castling. Stalemate is counted as a draw.
